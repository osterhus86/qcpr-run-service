Make the game yours

    Three unique logins for your team to access, manage, and monitor the game.

Data Analytics

Measure your impact

Every campaign generates rich CPR performance data. Your standard package includes exportable post-campaign data — live dashboards and custom analytics are available as upgrades.

Included
4 features
  • CPR performance metrics
    Score, compression depth, release, and rate — detailed per-session data for every player.
  • Volume data
    Total number of players, compressions performed, and time spent — a full picture of your campaign's reach.
  • Filterable by date & account
    Slice your data by campaign date or game account to compare results across events or locations.
  • Data export after campaign
    Export your full dataset up to 3 times after the campaign ends — ready for your own reporting or stakeholder presentations.
Optional Upgrades
3 add-ons
  • Live data (5-min delay)
    Monitor campaign performance in near real-time during your event — data refreshes every 5 minutes.
    Add-on
  • Self-serve dashboard access
    Log in to your own analytics dashboard anytime — explore results at your own pace without waiting for exports.
    Add-on
  • Custom filters & correlations
    Tailored data views with custom filters, additional data points, and cross-metric correlations to match your reporting needs.
